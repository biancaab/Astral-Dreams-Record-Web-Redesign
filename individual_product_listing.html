Description:

The unique performance of this talented and inspiring vocalist that this album offers, really inspired hard progressive tinged blue compositions and is considered as a rare album. His particular deep, slightly dirt and great voice is just a part of a great musical project where acoustic guitar of Davy Graham, electric guitar, organ as well as piano played by himself are the leaders. Its uncommon to find interesting lyrics along with an evolute and interesting progressive sound where song is sang with tenderness in the wonderfull "Thoughts".
